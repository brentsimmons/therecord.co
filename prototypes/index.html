Season #1 - Seattle Before the iPhone: John K. Doe

Recorded 10 May 2013 live and in person at The Omni Group’s lovely offices overlooking Lake Union in Seattle.

Download the m4a file.

John K. Doe, founder of Kafka Studios, LLC, is a long-time Mac developer most well-known for Metamorphosis (a bug-tracking app used by pretty much everybody). Much of his company’s financial success comes from niche apps: The Trial (legal software) and The Castle (estate planning).

Chris and Brent talk to John about the early days: how he got into Mac programming in the ’80s (accidentally, it turns out) and how he kept his calm during the “bealeagured” days in the ’90s. (Some speculate that pharmaceuticals were involved. John sets the record straight. Straight-ish. Sorta straight.)
